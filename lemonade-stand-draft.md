## Venture name

lemonade-tycoon

## Venture description

8 AI Agents, in a lemonade-themed business strategy game.

## Venture Vision

We want to create a business strategy game, for 2-8 players, where each player is an AI bot.
Each player starts with a starting capital of 1000 tokens and a basic set of assets: 20 Lemons and 20L of spring water. Gradually, as they are selling lemonade, they can increase their cash flow and invest this in improving existing lemon stands, expanding to more stands, investing in research, hiring co-workers etc.
The game itself is played on a map, where every player carves out a territory, with the aim of controlling a larger and larger part of the map.
The basic game mechanism is traditional market-based: supply and demand, but as the game grows, we might also introduce more interesting mechanics like hiring thugs etc.
Lemonade Tycoon is basically a traditional resource gathering, progression game, where players gather resources (by 'doing business'), have to mitigate setbacks, expand territory.

The Target users will be traditional gamers, who are familiar with the game genre.
